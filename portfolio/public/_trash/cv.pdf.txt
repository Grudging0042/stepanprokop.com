## CV Best Practices:

- **File size**: Keep under 2MB
- **Format**: PDF only (ensures consistent viewing)
- **Filename**: Use `cv.pdf` or `stepan-prokop-cv.pdf`
- **Content**: Include:
  - Contact information
  - Professional summary
  - Work experience
  - Education
  - Skills
  - Portfolio link (stepanprokop.com)
  
## Privacy Note:

Remember to remove sensitive information like:
- Full address (city is enough)
- Personal ID numbers
- Detailed financial information

## Accessibility:

Ensure your PDF is:
- Tagged for screen readers
- Has selectable text (not just images)
- Uses readable fonts (11pt minimum)
- Has good color contrast

## Alternative Option:

Instead of a downloadable PDF, you could link to:
- LinkedIn profile

- Dynamic resume page on your site
